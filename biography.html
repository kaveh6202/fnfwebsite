My Journey

From Student to Teacher

A path of continuous learning and growth.

2015

Early Beginnings

My journey into movement began with a personal need for healing and self-discovery. What started as a search for physical wellness became a profound exploration of mind-body connection.

2017-2019

Training & Certification

Extensive training in both yoga and Pilates methodologies, combined with ongoing education in anatomy, biomechanics, and holistic wellness approaches. Completed 500-hour yoga teacher training and comprehensive Pilates certification.

2020

Teaching Philosophy

Developed my core teaching philosophy: every body is unique, and every practice should honor that uniqueness. I believe in creating space for authentic movement and genuine self-discovery.

2021

Feather and Flow Launch

Founded Feather and Flow, combining my passion for movement with my vision of holistic wellness. Started offering both individual sessions and group classes.

Present

Continuing Growth

The learning never stops. I continue to study, practice, and evolve my approach, always seeking new ways to serve my students' wellness journeys. Currently expanding into stress relief and therapeutic movement.

Teaching Philosophy

"My teaching philosophy is rooted in respect for each student's journey. I believe classes should be structured by expertise so that everyone practices in a space suited to their level, free from comparison or pressure.

Adjustment is central to my approach, as yoga should adapt to the individual body rather than forcing the body into an ideal. I emphasize that yoga is not about constantly pushing limits but about showing up with mindfulness—because as long as you attend with presence, yoga is already working.

Through awareness in posture and presence on the mat, we nurture clarity, patience, and balance, which naturally extend into joy and a deeper sense of life beyond the practice."

— My Approach to Teaching
